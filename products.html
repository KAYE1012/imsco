Titanium Cranio-Maxillofacial Implant

Craniomaxillofacial implant systems are made from titanium and available in 1.2 mm, 1.6 mm, 2.0 mm, 2.3 mm and 2.7 mm. Due to the huge variety of plates and screws, our systems are suitable for every type of cranial and maxillofacial fixation. The different systems are color coded by size to avoid any mix- up or confusion.

  • Modular CMF Cluster
    consists of MICRO 1.2, MEDIUM 1.6, and MINI 2.0 for osteosynthesis in oral and maxillofacial surgery.
  • Mandible (Modular Lower Jaw System Cluster)
    includes FRACTURE, RECONSTRUCTION PLATE, and CONDYLAR PROSTHESIS for lower jaw reconstruction.

Why Choose Our Titanium Plating System?

  • Biocompatibility & Strength – Corrosion-resistant, lightweight, and durable.
  • Customizable Fixation – Available in various thicknesses and designs.
  • Color-Coded Systems – Easy identification of plates and screws.
  • High-Precision Screwdrivers – For accurate and easy handling.
  • Efficient Storage & Organization – Comes with dedicated storage trays.

Bioresorbable Implant

The Bioresorbable Implant System is engineered using advanced biodegradable co-polymers, including L-Lactide, D,L-Lactide, Polyglycolide, and Trimethylene Carbonate (TMC). These materials have a well-documented history of safe clinical use, ensuring high reliability in surgical applications.

  • Inion CPS™ System
    For adult patients’ use
    1.5 mm, 2.0 mm and 2.5 mm systems
    Mechanical strength retention: 70% of the initial strength 9 weeks after implantation Bioresorption time: 2-4 years
  • Inion CPS™ Baby System
    Mechanical strength retention: 70% of the initial strength 6 weeks after implantation Bioresorption time: 2-3 years
  • 2.0mm CPS SYSTEM
    For midface, mandibular and orthognatic fixation (similar use to titanium 1.5-1.7mm)
    Strength retention is minimum 9 weeks
  • 1.5mm CPS SYSTEM
    For cranial and midface fixation (similar use to titanium 1.0 - 1.2mm)
    Strength retention is minimum 9 weeks
  • 2.5mm CPS SYSTEM
    For mandibular fixation (similar use to titanium 2.0 -2.4mm)
    Strength retention is minimum 9 weeks

    Bone Scaffolding Meshes

    Our implants heal bone naturally. They are made of a bioresorbable polymer that dissolves over time, leaving only natural, healthy bone. This significantly reduces post-surgery complication rates compared to permanent implants.

    • Osteomesh in Orbital Floor Reconstruction
      Osteomesh® is designed and intended for use in repair of orbital floor fracture.

      • Osteoplug in Burr Hole Restoration
        Osteoplug® is designed and intended for use in repair of neurosurgical burr holes.

        • Dental Osteomesh
          Osteomesh® is a specially engineered 3D printed bioresorbable mesh designed to mimic the microarchitecture of natural bone.
        • Dental Osteoplug
          Osteoplug® is a conical shape 3D printed bioresorbable scaffold for fresh dental extraction sockets.

          Why Choose Osteopore?

          • Bioresorbable & Biodegradable – The material naturally dissolves, eliminating the need for secondary surgeries.
          • 3D-Printed Precision – Custom-made scaffolds designed to fit specific anatomical needs.
          • Enhanced Bone Regeneration – Porous structure encourages cell growth and faster healing.
          • Reduced Risk of Complications – Lower risk of infections and long-term implant-related issues.
