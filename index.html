Company Overview

Integra Medica Solutions Corp. (IMSco), established in 2016, is a trusted provider of advanced Oral and Cranio-Maxillofacial implant systems in the Philippines. We specialize in high-quality and accessible surgical solutions for maxillofacial trauma, mandibular fractures, bone reconstruction and related procedures.

IMSco is committed to delivering excellence through precision-engineered products and reliable surgical instruments tailored to the needs of healthcare professionals. Our offerings support safe, effective and innovative treatment approaches in both hospital and clinical settings.

Integra Medica Solutions Corp. is officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), ensuring our operations meet the highest standards of compliance and credibility.

Products & Services

Titanium Implant

Titanium Implant system includes a comprehensive range of components such as:

  • Micro Plates
  • Mini Plates
  • Fracture Plates
  • Reconstruction Plates
  • Condyle Prosthesis – a specialized solution for jaw and facial reconstruction, supporting both functional and aesthetic outcomes

These implants are engineered for all types of cranial and maxillofacial fixation. To ensure surgical efficiency and minimize errors, the systems are color-coded by size, offering clear differentiation during procedures.

Biodegradable Implant

Biodegradable polymer implants are designed to support faster and more natural bone healing. These implants gradually dissolve as the body heals, eliminating the need for a second surgery to remove them. Their excellent handling properties and safe degradation profile promote efficient tissue integration and recovery.

Made from advanced co-polymers like L-Lactide, D,L-Lactide, Polyglycolide, and Trimethylene Carbonate (TMC)—all clinically proven and widely used in medical applications.

Suitable for both adult and pediatric patients, the system offers:

  • Mechanical strength retention: ~70% after 6–9 weeks
  • Full resorption time: 2 to 4 years

Bioresorbable Implant

Made of bioresorbable polymer that dissolves over time, leaving only natural, healthy bone. This significantly reduces post-surgery complication rates compared to permanent implants.

  • Osteomesh® – for orbital floor reconstruction, Its porous interconnected microarchitecture demonstrates mechanical properties similar to human cancellous bone, providing a regenerative niche suitable for bone formation.
  • Osteoplug® – for is a regenerative implant used to fill bone gaps after craniotomies, minimizing pain and cosmetic issues. Made with Osteopore® technology, it promotes natural bone healing between the skull and bone flap.
  • Dental Osteomesh® – a 3D printed bioresorbable mesh that mimics bone microarchitecture
  • Dental Osteoplug® – a conical-shaped scaffold for fresh dental extraction sockets

Patient Specific Implant

The Patient Specific Implant (PSI) for mandibular bone reconstruction is a custom-designed 3D printed scaffold precisely tailored to match the patient’s unique anatomy.

Made from polycaprolactone (PCL), this implant is:

  • Bioresorbable – gradually absorbed by the body
  • Biocompatible – safe and integrates well with tissue
  • Non-toxic – clinically proven for long-term use

PSI offers precise anatomical fit and structural support, making it ideal for complex mandibular reconstructions.

Stereolithography

Our three-dimensional (3D) printing services are revolutionizing craniomaxillofacial surgery by enabling surgeons to visualize and plan procedures with enhanced precision and confidence.

We provide custom 3D-printed models for:

  • Trauma cases
  • Bone tumors
  • Bone deformities
These models improve surgical outcomes by offering a patient-specific anatomical reference, allowing for better pre-operative assessment and intraoperative guidance.
